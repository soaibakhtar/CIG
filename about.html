About Us

We Are Full Service Architecture Studio

Established in 2006, C.I.G Interior Design is a well-established and reputable interior design firm that has consistently redefined the art of interior and exterior design.

With a rich legacy of over a decade, we've successfully completed more than 250 projects, leaving a trail of satisfied clients who are living and working in spaces that epitomize their vision and aspirations.

Our Mission

Elevating Environments, Enhancing Lifestyles.

C.I.G Interior Design is to elevate living and working environments through innovative design concepts, thus enhancing the lifestyles of our clients.

We believe in the transformative power of design to create spaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and harmonious. Our dedication to excellence and creativity is reflected in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

Designing Dreams, Shaping Realities.

Our vision is to continue designing dreams and shaping the realities of our clients

We aspire to be at the forefront of the interior and exterior design industry, setting benchmarks for creativity, functionality, and sustainability. Through our work, we seek to inspire and set new standards in the world of design.
